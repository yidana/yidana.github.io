Interactive Census Exploration

The purpose of this research is to establish a web service for managing national statistical data which unlike a website provides services to the user. The web service will display population data and the various statistical interpretations that have been applied to the data that would be of significance to the lay person. The web service will also help business executives, researchers and students make analyses with the data available.
